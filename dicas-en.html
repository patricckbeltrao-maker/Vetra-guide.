📘 General Guide — Last War

Basic tips to grow faster in the game

🎮 Introduction

Last War: Survival Game is a strategy game where players must develop their base, strengthen heroes, train troops, and work as a team within an alliance to face enemies and participate in events.

To progress efficiently, it is important to understand how to manage resources, prioritize upgrades, and collaborate with your alliance.

This guide brings together key tips to help players grow faster and avoid common mistakes.

🏗️ Prioritize Base Growth

The Headquarters (HQ) is the most important building in the game. It determines:

  • The maximum level of buildings
  • The level of your troops
  • Access to new features

💡 Tip: Whenever possible, plan your upgrades to level up your HQ as quickly as possible.

To do this:

  • Upgrade required buildings
  • Save enough resources
  • Prepare the necessary research

The higher your HQ level, the stronger your account becomes.

🔬 Keep Research Active

The Research Lab is one of the most important buildings for account growth.

Research can improve:

  • Resource production
  • Construction speed
  • Troop power
  • Event efficiency

💡 Strong players always keep research running.

Never let your lab stay idle.

🧑‍🚀 Upgrade Your Heroes Strategically

Heroes are essential for:

  • Battles
  • Events
  • Base defense
  • Map attacks

Important tips:

  • Focus first on your main squad heroes
  • Upgrade hero skills
  • Improve equipment
  • Level them up whenever possible

💡 Investing in too many heroes at once can slow your progress. Focus on a core group.

🪖 Train Troops Constantly

Troops are essential to:

  • Attack enemies
  • Defend your base
  • Participate in events
  • Capture structures

Best practices:

  • Never leave your barracks idle
  • Always keep troops in training
  • Balance quantity and troop level

A strong army makes a big difference in alliance events.

🤝 Be Active in Your Alliance

A good alliance greatly boosts your progress.

Within an alliance you can:

  • Receive help with constructions
  • Join collective events
  • Earn extra rewards
  • Receive reinforcements

💡 Active players in strong alliances grow much faster.

🏆 Participate in Game Events

Events are one of the best ways to obtain:

  • Resources
  • Speedups
  • Hero fragments
  • Rare items

Main events include:

  • VS (Alliance Duel)
  • Desert Battle
  • Zombie Siege
  • Weekly events

💡 Saving resources to use during events can yield much better rewards.

📈 Resource Management

Managing resources well is essential.

Important tips:

  • Avoid spending everything at once
  • Save resource items for events
  • Use storage to protect production
  • Plan large upgrades in advance

Good management prevents you from running out of resources at critical moments.

⚔️ Strategy and Planning

Last War is a strategy game. Players who plan well tend to progress faster.

Key strategies:

  • Plan upgrades before events
  • Save speedups
  • Coordinate attacks with your alliance
  • Prioritize upgrades that increase overall power
💡 Organized players achieve more rewards and faster growth.

⭐ Conclusion

Progress in Last War: Survival Game depends on consistency, planning, and teamwork.

By following these tips, you will be able to:

  • Grow your base faster
  • Strengthen your heroes
  • Build a stronger army
  • Contribute more to your alliance

With dedication and strategy, your power in the game will continuously increase.
