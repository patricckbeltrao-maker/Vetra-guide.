🏜️ Desert Battle

Vetra Alliance Guide

🏜️ What is Desert Battle

Desert Battle is an alliance event where players enter a special desert map. The main objective is to capture and hold strategic structures to generate points for your alliance until the end of the match.

During the event, alliances fight for control of important locations. The longer your alliance holds these structures, the more points it earns.

🎯 Event Objective

The main goal is to accumulate more points than the enemy alliance before time runs out.

To achieve this, players must:

  • Capture structures on the map
  • Defend captured points
  • Attack enemy players
  • Work together with the alliance

Coordination between members is essential to gain a strategic advantage.

🏗️ Strategic Points

On the Desert Battle map there are several structures that generate points for the alliance that controls them. These usually include:

  • Forward Bases
  • Control Centers
  • Main Map Structures

The more structures your alliance controls, the higher your total score will be.

🤝 Importance of Teamwork

Unlike normal battles in the game, Desert Battle heavily depends on coordination between alliance members.

Some players need to:

  • Attack enemy bases ⚔️
  • Defend captured structures 🛡️
  • Reinforce allies during battles 🤝

When the alliance is well organized, the chances of victory increase significantly.

🏆 Rewards

At the end of the event, players receive rewards based on alliance performance and individual participation.

These rewards may include:

  • Resources
  • Upgrade items
  • Event points
  • Other special rewards
💡 Actively participating in Desert Battle is a great way to strengthen your account and help your alliance grow in the game.

🏗️ Map Structures in Desert Battle

1️⃣ Forward Base

Function: Serves as a support point for attacks and defense.

Importance: The more forward bases your alliance controls, the easier it becomes to maintain territory and reinforce troops quickly.

Tip: Defend it well, because losing a forward base can allow the enemy to advance quickly.

2️⃣ Control Center

Function: A structure that generates continuous points for the alliance that controls it.

Importance: It is the main source of points during the battle.

Tip: Prioritize capturing control centers but maintain enough defense to avoid losing them easily.

3️⃣ Main Structures

Function: Key buildings that strengthen the alliance's presence on the map.

Importance: Controlling these structures provides bonus points and strategic advantage over the enemy.

Tip: Coordinate team attacks to capture these structures quickly.

4️⃣ Watch Towers

Function: Provide map vision and detect enemy movements.

Importance: Help with attack and defense strategy.

Tip: Capturing watch towers helps plan ambushes and protect critical areas.

💡 Strategic Summary

  • Prioritize control centers to gain points faster
  • Maintain forward bases for reinforcements and mobility
  • Capture main structures to gain long-term advantages
  • Use watch towers to monitor enemies

The secret to success in Desert Battle is capturing and holding these structures as a team while balancing attack and defense. ⚔️🛡️
