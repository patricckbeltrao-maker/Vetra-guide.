🔬 Research in Last War

Research is one of the most important parts of progression in the game. It works as permanent upgrades for your account, increasing troop strength, base efficiency, and development speed.

Unlike other upgrades, research affects your entire army and economy, so investing in it properly greatly accelerates your progress.

When done correctly, research helps in three main areas:

  • ⚔️ More military power (stronger and tougher troops)
  • 🏗️ Faster base development
  • 📈 Greater efficiency in events and PvP/PvE battles

That’s why stronger players usually have well-developed research trees.

📊 Recommended research order

To progress in a balanced way, it’s ideal to follow a priority order between research types.

1️⃣ Development / Economy

📌 First priority

These researches improve:

  • Construction speed
  • Research speed
  • Resource production
  • Gathering capacity
💡 This accelerates everything else in the game.

Recommended percentage:
➡️ 40% of your research

2️⃣ Military

📌 Second priority

This tree contains combat upgrades such as:

  • Troop attack
  • Troop defense
  • Troop health
  • Battle efficiency

These researches directly increase the power of your squad.

Recommended percentage:
➡️ 35% of your research

3️⃣ Troops

📌 Medium priority

In this tree you improve:

  • Unlocking higher troop tiers
  • Faster troop training
  • Better unit efficiency

Having stronger troops makes a big difference in wars and events.

Recommended percentage:
➡️ 15% of your research

4️⃣ Specialized / Advanced

📌 Final priority

These are more specific researches, usually focused on:

  • Situational bonuses
  • Advanced combat improvements
  • Strategic advantages

They are usually done after the main research trees are well developed.

Recommended percentage:
➡️ 10% of your research

🎯 Important tip

Always try to keep research running at all times. Never leave your laboratory idle.

Strong players usually follow this logic:

  • Research speed upgrades first
  • Then military improvements
  • Then troop upgrades and specific bonuses

This creates steady power growth.

✅ Ideal distribution summary

  • 1º Desenvolvimento - 80% a 90%
  • 2º Economia - 40%

  • 4º Unidades - 60% a 80%
