Vetra Alliance Guide

🏗️ Building Priority Order

1️⃣ Headquarters (HQ)

This is the most important building in the game.

It:

  • Unlocks new building levels
  • Unlocks stronger troops
  • Allows you to progress in the game
💡 Always prioritize upgrading your HQ whenever possible.

2️⃣ Research Laboratory

This is where you perform research that increases your power.

It improves:

  • Troops
  • Economy
  • Construction speed
  • Military strength

📌 The higher the laboratory level, the better research you unlock.

3️⃣ Training Center / Barracks

Responsible for training your troops.

Upgrading these buildings gives you:

  • Stronger troops
  • Faster training
  • Higher troop capacity

⚔️ This directly increases your squad power.

4️⃣ Hero Center

Improves the development of your heroes.

Helps to:

  • Strengthen squads
  • Improve battle performance
  • Increase efficiency in events

🦸 Strong heroes make a big difference in combat.

5️⃣ Squad Building

Increases the capacity and efficiency of your squads.

Benefits:

  • More troops in the squad
  • Better battle performance
  • More overall power

6️⃣ Warehouse

Protects resources from enemy attacks.

📦 Very important to avoid losing:

  • Gold
  • Food
  • Other resources

Especially when you start participating in wars.

7️⃣ Resource Production

Includes buildings such as:

  • Farms
  • Mines
  • Refineries

They help maintain a constant flow of resources.

🏥 Hospitals

Hospitals are used to:

  • ❤️ Heal wounded troops
  • 🛡️ Prevent permanent troop deaths
  • ⚔️ Reduce losses during wars

The higher the hospital level:

  • The greater the wounded troop capacity
  • More troops can be recovered
