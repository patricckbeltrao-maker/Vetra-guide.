🪖 Marshal Event

Alliance Guide

🪖 What is the Marshal Event

The Marshal Event is a collective alliance event. A leader or officer can summon the Marshal, which works as a special boss that all alliance members can attack.

During the event, players use their squads to deal as much damage as possible. At the end, rewards are distributed based on participation.

🔑 Key Donations

Before summoning the Marshal, it is necessary to donate keys.

These keys are contributions from alliance members and are required to unlock the boss summon. Once the required number of keys is reached, a leader or officer can activate the event.

Donating keys is important because it:

  • 🤝 Helps the alliance activate the event more often
  • 🎁 Allows all members to receive rewards
  • 📈 Contributes to the alliance's collective growth
💡 For this reason, it is recommended that all members donate their keys whenever possible.

⚔️ How the Battle Works

After the Marshal is summoned:

  • 1️⃣ Alliance members can attack the boss
  • 2️⃣ Each attack deals accumulated damage
  • 3️⃣ Total damage determines ranking positions
  • 4️⃣ At the end, everyone receives rewards based on participation

Even players with lower power should participate, since any amount of damage guarantees rewards.

🎁 Event Rewards

The Marshal Event can give several rewards, such as:

  • Resources
  • Speedups
  • Evolution items
  • Reward chests

These items help a lot in developing your base, heroes, and troops.

🧠 Important Tips

To make the most of the event:

  • ⚡ Use your strongest squad
  • 🔑 Donate keys whenever possible
  • 🪖 Attack the Marshal every time available
  • 🤝 Always participate when the event is active
🏆 The more active the alliance is in this event, the more rewards all members can earn over time.
