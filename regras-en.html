📜 Alliance Rules — Last War: Survival Game

To maintain a strong, organized, and competitive alliance, it is essential that all members follow some basic rules. They ensure collective growth, good coexistence, and better results in events.

Below are the main rules and the importance of each:

⚔️ Event Participation

All members must participate in the main game events, such as:

  • VS (Versus)
  • Marshal
  • Zombie Siege
  • Desert Battle

📌 Why is this important?
Events are the main source of rewards and alliance progression. When everyone participates, the alliance grows faster and achieves better rankings.

🔑 Make Donations

Contributing with donations (such as keys and other resources) is essential.

📌 Importance:
Donations allow events to be activated and alliance structures to be improved, benefiting all members.

The video shows how to make donations and how this directly helps the entire alliance. Donate where you see the 👍 mark.

🛡️ Shield Usage (Friday at 22:59)

Everyone must activate their shield at this time.

📌 Importance:
Prevents enemy attacks, especially before important events or risky periods.

This video shows how to activate the shield, avoid troop losses, and where to buy it.

🚂 Train Participation

Whenever possible, participate in the train and donate your tickets.

📌 Importance:
Ensures extra rewards and strengthens the alliance's collective progress.

🤝 Help with the “hand”

Always click to help whenever the option appears.

📌 Importance:
Reduces construction and research time for allies, accelerating everyone's growth.

Here you can see how helping allies directly impacts collective growth.

🎯 Participate in rallies

Join rallies whenever possible.

📌 Importance:
Rallies are essential to defeat stronger enemies and obtain better rewards.

The video demonstrates how to join rallies and contribute to bigger victories.

🐝 Stay in the hive (meeting point)

Keep your base close to the alliance.

📌 Importance:
Facilitates help, defense, rally participation, and overall alliance organization.

This video shows how to stay close to the alliance and where to buy teleport.

📢 Read alliance announcements and polls

Always stay attentive to announcements and respond when necessary.

📌 Importance:
Keeps everyone aligned with strategies, events, and important decisions.

The video shows where to access announcements and their importance.

✉️ Read president messages

Follow the correspondence sent by the president.

📌 Importance:
These messages usually contain important instructions for the entire alliance.

Here you learn where to view leadership messages.

🚫 Respect NAP (Very important)

The NAP (non-aggression pact) must be strictly followed.

📌 Importance:
Avoids unnecessary conflicts and maintains peace between alliances and players.

🤝 Respect among members

Always be polite and respectful.

📌 Importance:
A healthy environment improves cooperation and everyone's experience in the game.

🚫 Prohibition of improper looting

Do not loot:

  • Tasks
  • Trucks
  • Harvests from server 1781

📌 Importance:
Avoids punishments, conflicts, and maintains the alliance's good reputation.

The video shows how to loot in other servers. NOTE: keep the option "Remove current War Zone from loot selection" checked to avoid problems.

⚠️ Do not attack server players

Do not attack server members, except:

  • Farm accounts
  • Players without alliance

📌 Importance:
Maintains server organization and avoids unnecessary wars.

🏆 Conclusion

Following these rules is not just an obligation, but a way to strengthen the alliance and ensure everyone's growth.

An organized, active, and respectful alliance will always be more successful within Last War: Survival Game. 🚀
