🧟 Zombie Siege

Alliance Guide

🧟 What is Zombie Siege

Zombie Siege is an event where hordes of zombies attack alliance members' bases in several waves.

During the event:

  • Zombies attack your base directly
  • Some waves may attack allied bases
  • Players must defend using their troops and heroes

The more waves you successfully defend, the better the rewards will be.

🏰 Placing Troops on the Wall

Before the event starts, it is important to place troops to defend the base wall.

When you position troops on the wall:

  • 🛡️ Your base can defend itself from zombie attacks
  • ⚔️ Your troops and heroes fight automatically
  • 📈 You have a better chance of surviving more waves

If there are no troops defending the wall, your base may quickly lose to the zombies.

⚠️ Removing Troops After the Event

A very important tip is to remove the troops from the wall after the event ends.

This is recommended because:

  • Other players may attack your base
  • Your troops could die in unnecessary battles
  • You may lose soldiers for no reason

After Zombie Siege ends, the ideal action is to remove the troops from the wall and place them back into your squads.

🤝 Helping Allies

During the event, it is also possible to help alliance members whose bases are being attacked by zombies.

This helps to:

  • 🛡️ Better defend the alliance
  • 📈 Increase overall alliance performance
  • 🎁 Secure better rewards for everyone

🎁 Rewards

Zombie Siege can provide several rewards such as:

  • Resources
  • Speedups
  • Evolution items
  • Reward chests

The more waves you and your alliance survive, the better the rewards will be.

💡 Strategy Summary

  • 1️⃣ Place troops on the wall before the event starts
  • 2️⃣ Use your strongest heroes and troops to defend
  • 3️⃣ Help allies whenever possible
  • 4️⃣ Remove troops from the wall after the event to avoid unnecessary losses
By following these tips, you can defend your base better and avoid losing soldiers unnecessarily. 🛡️⚔️
