🦸 Upgrading Heroes

Vetra Alliance Guide

🦸 What does upgrading heroes mean

Upgrading heroes means increasing their level using experience (EXP). The higher a hero’s level:

  • ⚔️ Higher damage
  • 🛡️ Higher defense
  • ❤️ More health
  • 💥 More effective abilities

High level heroes make your squad much stronger, both for PvE (zombies, events) and PvP (battles against other players).

💡 Tip: try to keep the heroes in your main squad at the maximum level allowed by your Headquarters (HQ).

⭐ Which heroes to focus on first

At the beginning of the game, it is not recommended to spend resources on every hero. Focus on building one main squad.

Priority is usually:

  • 1️⃣ Legendary heroes (orange) – the strongest heroes in the game
  • 2️⃣ Epic heroes (purple) – good until you obtain stronger legendary heroes
  • 3️⃣ Rare or common heroes – usually only used at the start

It is also important to consider the hero’s troop type (tank, shooter, etc.) to build a balanced squad.

💡 Common strategy: focus on 3 to 5 main heroes and upgrade them first instead of spreading resources across many heroes.

🛡️ Hero equipment

Each hero can use equipment that greatly increases their attributes.

Equipment can increase:

  • Attack
  • Defense
  • Health
  • Overall squad power

It is important to:

  • 🔧 Upgrade equipment
  • ⭐ Improve their rarity
  • 🔁 Always equip the best items on your main squad
💡 Many players upgrade the main squad’s equipment first because the power increase is very significant.

🔥 Upgrading hero skills

Each hero has special abilities that can deal damage, buff allies, or weaken enemies.

Upgrading skills increases:

  • 💥 Ability damage
  • 🛡️ Bonuses for the squad
  • ⚡ Battle efficiency

Since skill resources are usually limited, the best approach is to:

  • Prioritize skills of your main heroes
  • Upgrade abilities that deal damage or buff your team first

🧠 Recommended strategy

To upgrade heroes efficiently:

  • 1️⃣ Choose a main squad
  • 2️⃣ Focus on strong legendary or epic heroes
  • 3️⃣ Upgrade the levels of your main heroes first
  • 4️⃣ Improve the equipment of your main squad
  • 5️⃣ Invest in the most important abilities

This will increase your power much faster and improve your performance in events such as VS, alliance wars, and map battles. ⚔️
